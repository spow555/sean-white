While on Deployment, I was awarded with a coin from the 38th Infantry Division Commanding General. Here is the statement for the coin:

SPC Oconnor executes his duties as an Information System Specialist with exceptional performance. SPC Oconnor's experience and dedication to his duties became an example for his peers to follow. His dedication to Helpdesk, Command Post Node, and Secure/Non-Secure access point operations were instrumental to TF Javelin’s mission in support of Operation Spartan Shield. He was able to identify issues within the task force helpdesk and created standard operation procedures that assisted in the tracking of over 750 Remedy tickets; ensuring all users on the Udari Flight-line were fully mission capable on SIPR/NIPR workstations and VOIP phones. SPC Oconnor is a WIN-T subject matter expert within TF Javelin, he trained the TF Javelin S6 section in SNAP operations and maintenance. Which ensured the tasked force had multiple operators to maintain the tactical system. He also assisted sister BNs on their WIN-T assets in Iraq. He isolated issues within the SNAP and Tek-V in location AAAB & AQ; troubleshot the issues bringing online those systems that were mission critical to medevac operations. His commitment to TF Javelin’s mission exemplifies the quality of signal Soldiers the Army has within it's ranks.
